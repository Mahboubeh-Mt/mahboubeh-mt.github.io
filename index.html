Turning data into decisions

I’m an accomplished Data Scientist with 5+ years of experience applying machine learning and statistics to large, multi-source datasets in healthcare, e-commerce, and finance. My strengths include data querying and cleaning, feature engineering, forecasting, and building regression/logit models that translate complex data into clear decisions for both technical and non-technical teams. I’m hands-on with Python/SQL, AWS, Docker, MLflow, and CI/CD, and experienced with structured/unstructured data, Databricks (Spark), and model interpretability across the full model lifecycle.

I’m also a highly analytical PhD candidate focused on medical imaging and AI. I bring expertise in machine learning, deep learning, NLP, and LLMs across diverse domains — from healthcare and e-commerce to clickstream, geomatics, and location-based user data. My research explores MRI-based neuroimaging with large multimodal datasets like UK Biobank and ADNI. I’m adept at end-to-end AI pipelines: robust data integration, model training, and scalable deployment on AWS and Azure. With skills in Python, TensorFlow, PyTorch, Power BI, plus experience in MLflow, GitLab CI/CD, GitHub, and Docker, I thrive on collaboration and problem-solving, turning clinical research into production-ready solutions.
